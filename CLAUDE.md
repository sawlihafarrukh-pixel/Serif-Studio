# Serif Studio — Claude Code Context

## Who is Serif Studio

Serif Studio is a one-man branding agency founded and run by a designer based in Malaysia with family roots in Pakistan. The studio specialises in brand identity design for startups, small businesses and lifestyle brands. The founder also runs a personal art and painting Instagram account with ~1000 followers which is used to cross-promote Serif Studio content.

## Mission

To make studio-quality branding accessible to startups and small businesses — not cheap, but accessible. Serif Studio democratises quality branding that was previously only available to big-budget clients.

## Positioning

- **Niche:**  lifestyle brands — jewellery, skincare, fashion, F&B
- **Markets:** Malaysia, Pakistan, and globally
- **Unique angle:** Culturally fluent across Southeast Asian and South Asian markets
- **Model:** One designer, no outsourcing — the person you speak to is the person doing the work

## Brand Identity

### Name

Serif Studio (Instagram: @serifstudioco)

### Logo

Star/compass mark in rust orange. Clean, minimal, memorable.

### Colour Palette

- Steel blue: #7BA3BC
- Warm cream: #F5EFD0
- Rust orange: #8B3A0F
- Dark brown: #3C2B24
- Off white: #F5F2EE

### Brand Voice

- Human, witty, direct
- Short captions — never long or corporate
- Casual tone, like texting a smart friend
- Never sounds like a brochure

### Tone Rules

- Always lowercase in captions
- Short punchy lines
- No fluff, no filler
- Every caption should be able to be read in under 10 seconds

## Portfolio Projects

### 1. Maeven Studio — Fine Jewellery

- **Direction:** Refined, sentimental, timeless luxury
- **Deliverables:** Script MM monogram, four logo colour variations, custom packaging design
- **Palette:** Warm neutrals — taupe, cream, black, gold
- **Tagline:** Understated Luxury
- **Notes:** Strongest portfolio piece. Highest engagement on Instagram.

### 2. Brunch Out — F&B Café

- **Direction:** Modern-rustic, warm artisanal comfort with clean simplicity
- **Deliverables:** Primary logo in three colourways, icon mark (L-shaped continuous line), packaging (cup mockup), menu design
- **Palette:** Dark brown, olive green, cream, yellow
- **Taglines:** “Less, but better” / “Form follows function”
- **Icon:** Continuous L-shaped line with upward curved tail — derived from the wordmark’s signature underline

### 3. Loa & Faye — Skincare

- **Direction:** Gentle, caring, poetic, aesthetically driven
- **Deliverables:** Primary logo, secondary logo, colour palette, packaging label (Gentle Glow Serum), Instagram post templates, Instagram profile mockup
- **Palette:** Blush pink, mauve, cream, dark charcoal
- **Tagline:** “Soft radiance for everyday skin”
- **Notes:** Instagram mockup slide is strongest individual asset across all projects

### 4. Sanis Shoes — Footwear (Real Client — Family Business)

- **Direction:** Quiet luxury, grounded, feminine
- **Chosen option:** Option 1 — warm brown palette
- **Deliverables:** Primary logo (The Seasons font), SS monogram, colour palette, brand guidelines
- **Palette:** #B4A08E, #3C2B24, #F6F2EE, #C9A3A3
- **Taglines:** “Quiet Luxury, Loud Comfort” / “Whole. Sound. Grounded.”
- **Typography:** The Seasons (display) + Montserrat (body)
- **Status:** First real paying client. Instagram page being built.
- **Notes:** Brand name comes from Latin meaning “whole” and “sound”
