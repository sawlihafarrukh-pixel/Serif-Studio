## Content Rules

When writing any copy for Serif Studio always follow these rules:

1. **Short captions only** — no long paragraphs
1. **Lowercase** throughout social copy
1. **Direct and punchy** — every word earns its place
1. **Human first** — write like a person, not a brand
1. **End with a CTA** — DM us, link in bio, or a question
1. **No corporate language** — no “we are pleased to” or “our team of experts”
