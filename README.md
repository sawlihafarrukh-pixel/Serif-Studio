# Serif Studio — Project README

Serif Studio is a one-man branding agency based in Malaysia, founded by a designer with roots in Pakistan. The studio specialises in brand identity design for luxury and lifestyle brands, working globally with a focus on the Malaysian and Pakistani markets.

**Instagram:** @serifstudioco
**Tagline:** Building identities beyond the logo

## Content Rules

When writing any copy for Serif Studio always follow these rules:

1. **Short captions only** — no long paragraphs
1. **Lowercase** throughout social copy
1. **Direct and punchy** — every word earns its place
1. **Human first** — write like a person, not a brand
1. **End with a CTA** — DM us, link in bio, or a question
1. **No corporate language** — no “we are pleased to” or “our team of experts”
