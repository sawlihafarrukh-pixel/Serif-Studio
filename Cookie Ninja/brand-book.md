## 01 — The Brand

**Name.** Cookie Ninja
**Category.** Single-product artisan bakery — Malaysia
**Founded.** 2026

**The Promise.**
One cookie. Stupidly well-made. Delivered in silence.

**The Mission.**
We don't make a menu. We make a strike. Cookie Ninja exists to prove that one product done with obsessive focus beats ten products done okay. Soft middle. Crisp edge. No compromise.

## 03 — Brand Voice

**Personality.**
A ninja who happens to bake. Quiet, focused, slightly cocky. Never tries too hard. Never explains too much.

**Tone Rules.**

- always lowercase in captions
- short punchy lines
- under 10 seconds to read
- no fluff, no filler
- self-aware humour, never corporate

**Vocabulary — say:**
strike · drop · vanish · deliver · mission · dojo · blade · silent · sharp · soft middle · crisp edge · the cookie

**Vocabulary — never:**
yummy · tasty · delish · please · crafted with love · supercharged · amazing · literally the best

**Signature Lines.**

- soft middle. crisp edge.
- sharp on delivery.
- silent delivery.
- the cookie strikes back.
- you didn't see us coming.
- no menu. no chaos.
- one cookie. one mission.

## 04 — Logo System

**Primary Mark.**
Stick-figure ninja in a dynamic throwing pose, releasing a small round cookie. In the full wordmark lockup, the dot of the "i" in "Ninja" is replaced with the ninja figure.

**Wordmark.**
"Cookie Ninja" set in *Recoleta Regular*. Two lines stacked, or one line wide. Tracking: 0.

**Variations.**

1. **Primary lockup** — wordmark + ninja icon
2. **Wordmark only** — for narrow spaces
3. **Icon only** — app icons, wax seals, packaging stamps
4. **Compass-star secondary mark** — alternate use only

**Clear Space.**
Minimum clearance on all sides equal to the width of the "C" in "Cookie".

**Minimum Sizing.**

- Print: 20mm width
- Digital: 80px width

**Don'ts.**

- never stretch, skew, or rotate
- never recolour outside the palette
- never add drop shadows, glows, or bevels
- never place on busy photography without a flat overlay
- never recreate the ninja figure freehand — always use the master file

| Role | Typeface | Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Display** | Recoleta Regular / Medium | Logo, hero headlines |
| **Accent** | Shippori Mincho | Sub-headlines, single-word punctuation |
| **Body** | DM Sans Regular | Captions, body copy, social |
| **Hand** | Brush-stroke (Reey or similar) | Illustrated tags, stamps only |

### Hierarchy

- **H1** — Recoleta Medium · 48pt · -1% tracking
- **H2** — Shippori Mincho Regular · 24pt
- **Body** — DM Sans Regular · 14pt · 160% line height
- **Caption** — DM Sans Regular · 12pt · all lowercase

## 07 — Photography & Illustration

### Direction

Cookie Ninja avoids polished food photography. The visual world is **dimensional flat illustration** — premium, intimate, slightly cinematic.

### Illustration Style Spec

- Dimensional flat illustration — soft volume, flat colour fills, no gradients inside shapes
- Single warm light source from the **upper-left**
- Subtle radial cream glow in the upper-left corner of the frame
- Soft cast shadows beneath every object
- Rust-orange four-point sparkle accent in the **lower-right** corner of every hero illustration
- No linework, no grain, no halftone
- 1:1 square for grid posts

### Hero Scene Library

Each scene is generated using existing product photography as the reference cookie.

1. **The Slice** — katana cutting cookie clean in half
2. **The Throw** — cookie mid-flight, motion lines
3. **The Stack** — three cookies stacked on dark plate
4. **Behind the Mask** — hands kneading dough, no face
5. **The Pull** — two hands snapping cookie, chocolate stretch
6. **The Squadron** — 3×3 grid of cookies, training-mat layout
7. **The Vault** — kraft box opening with rust light beam
8. **The Menu** — wooden plaque, one item
9. **The CTA** — cookie with hanging "dm ninja" tag

### Photography Rules (when used at all)

- only reuse the legacy dark stacked-cookies shot
- never use shots with pink ribbons, glass jars, dried flowers, or light backgrounds
- never show faces or full bodies — hands and silhouettes only

## 08 — Packaging

**Box.**
Kraft brown rectangular box with a top window. Single cookie or small stack visible.

**Closure.**
Thin black waxed twine, tied in a single neat knot.

**Seal.**
Circular rust-orange wax seal stamped with the star/compass mark, applied over the twine on the front face.

**Don'ts.**

- no pink ribbons (legacy — archived)
- no plastic film wrap
- no bows, no decorative tags except the brand tag
- no patterned tissue paper

## 09 — Social Strategy

**Platforms.** Instagram primary · TikTok secondary

**Grid Aesthetic.**
Every post uses an illustration from the hero library, the legacy dark stacked photo, or a pure design slide. No raw product photography.

**Content Pillars.**

1. Hero scenes (the illustration library)
2. Manifesto / world-building
3. Engagement (questions, name-the-drop)
4. CTA / drop posts

**Cadence.** 4 posts per week. Best time: **8–9pm MYT**.

**Caption Rules.**

- lowercase always
- max 3 lines
- one CTA per post — or none
- emojis sparingly — only 🥷 📦 ⚔️
