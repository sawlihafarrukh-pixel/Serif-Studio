# Sanis Shoes — Three More Launch Posters (Vol. 2)

Three additional poster concepts for Sanis, designed to extend the campaign launched in *sanis-launch-posters.md*. These three lean into **figure-in-context** and **aerial composition** — three angles deliberately absent from the prior set and the IG launch sequence.

This document inherits the universal rules from `sanis-launch-posters.md`. The updated figure rule is reproduced below.

---

## Updated Universal Rule (applies retroactively to all posters and IG content)

**No faces, necks, or chests. Ever.** Hands and ankles are permitted at any time. If a figure appears in the frame with the shoe, it must be **fully clothed in modest dress** — long sleeves, wide-leg jeans or long skirts, no exposed skin above the elbow. Frame **strictly below the chest, ideally below the knees or thighs.** No jewellery, no nail polish, no styled forearms.

All other universal rules — sealed palette, sentence case, natural light, no text in image-gen prompts, 1200 × 1800 working canvas — carry over from the prior document.

### Visual brief

Full-bleed editorial photograph, side-angle, mid-stride. **Frame strictly from below the knee down — no torso, no chest, no face, no neck, nothing above the knee visible.** A woman walking from right to left across the frame in mid-step: one warm-taupe Sanis round-toe ballet flat planted on the floor, the other lifted mid-stride. She wears **wide-leg dark-wash denim jeans** that fall cleanly to the ankle, hemmed just at the top of the shoe — fully covered, modest. Floor surface: **warm-taupe polished limestone or honed marble** (Karachi domestic flooring), with a subtle directional grain. Background: a warm-taupe lime-washed wall, slightly out of focus. **Late-afternoon raking window light from camera right**, producing one soft long shadow across the floor behind the planted foot. Slight motion in the lifted foot — not blurred, but the moment-of-step feel. Shot at 35mm, slight low angle, f/4. Slightly warm exposure, natural light only, no flash. **No text overlays in the image.**

### Modesty checklist (apply before approving any generated image)

- [ ] No face visible anywhere in the frame
- [ ] No neck visible
- [ ] No chest visible
- [ ] If a body is present, framing cuts off at or below the chest (ideally below the knee)
- [ ] All visible skin is below the elbow on the arms and below the knee on the legs
- [ ] All clothing is full-coverage — long sleeves, long pants or skirt
- [ ] No jewellery on visible hands
- [ ] No nail polish, no styled forearms

If any item fails, regenerate the image. Image-gen models drift toward exposed skin and partial faces — be ready to re-prompt 2–3 times to land a clean result.

### Common re-prompt fixes

| Problem | Fix |
|---|---|
| Face slipped into frame | Add "camera framing is BELOW the knee only; nothing above the knee appears in the frame" |
| Sleeve too short / wrist skin showing | Add "long-sleeve cardigan/shirt with sleeve falling past the wrist" |
| Jeans too tight or ankle showing | Add "wide-leg loose denim, hemmed cleanly at the top of the shoe, ankle fully covered" |
| Floor / wall colour drifted | Add the specific hex codes and "Karachi domestic interior" |
| Extra props appeared | Add "ONLY the shoes, the textile, and the muslin appear in the frame" |
